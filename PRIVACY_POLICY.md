# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: December 12, 2025**

## Overview

TryLab ("we," "our," or "us") operates an A/B testing and conversion optimization application for Shopify merchants. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application and related services (collectively, our "Services").

By using our Services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our Services.

**California Residents:** This policy includes information required by the California Consumer Privacy Act ("CCPA"). California-specific rights are detailed in the "Your Rights" section below.

---

## Scope

This Privacy Policy covers:

- Information collected through our Shopify application
- Data received from Shopify's APIs when you use our Services
- Information you provide directly to us
- Data collected automatically through your use of our Services

This Privacy Policy does not cover:

- Information collected outside of our Services
- Third-party websites or services that may link to our Services
- Information collected by Shopify or other third parties independently

---

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use our Services, you may provide us with:

- Account information (name, email address, business details)
- Communication preferences
- Feedback and support requests
- Payment and billing information (processed through Shopify's billing system)

### Information from Shopify

When you install and use our application, we access certain data through Shopify's APIs to provide our Services:

- **Product Information:** Product data, variants, and inventory information needed to run A/B tests
- **Theme Data:** Theme files and templates to implement test variants
- **Order Data:** Transaction information to measure conversion rates and test performance
- **Store Settings:** Basic store configuration relevant to A/B testing functionality

**Important:** We do not collect personally identifiable customer information (names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses) from Shopify's APIs. Our Services focus on product and storefront optimization, not individual customer data.

### Automatically Collected Information

When you interact with our Services, we automatically collect:

- **Technical Data:** IP address, browser type, device information, operating system
- **Usage Data:** How you interact with our application, features used, time spent
- **Performance Data:** Application performance metrics, error logs, system diagnostics
- **Location Data:** General geographic location derived from IP address (country/region level only)

### Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

- Maintain your session and authentication state
- Remember your preferences and settings
- Analyze how our Services are used
- Improve functionality and user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings, though disabling certain cookies may limit functionality.
